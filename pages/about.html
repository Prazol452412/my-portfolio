About Me

I am a Bachelor of Information Technology student at Model Institute of Technology (MIT), Kathmandu, affiliated with International American University. I split my time between cybersecurity, cloud computing, and digital marketing.

On the security side, I play CTFs on HackTheBox and study both offensive and defensive techniques. I am AWS Cloud Security Foundation certified and slowly building toward more cloud security credentials.

By day, I run Meta advertising campaigns at Vidhya Bodhi Education Pvt. Ltd., an education consultancy in Kathmandu, generating leads through social media. I enjoy the contrast between packets and pixels, exploits and engagement metrics.

Break things to understand them. Build things to prove it.
